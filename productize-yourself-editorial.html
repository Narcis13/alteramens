    The Framework

    "Productize has specific knowledge and leverage. Yourself has uniqueness and accountability. So you can combine all of these pieces into these two words."

    — Naval Ravikant

    4 core components. 10 principles. One path to wealth.

    01
    Principle

    Play Compounding Games

    "All the returns in life come from compound interest."

    — Naval

    • Pick a game worth playing long-term
    • Choose an industry you love, people you want to grow with
    • Every direction change interrupts compounding
    02
    Principle

    Specific Knowledge

    "Knowledge society can train you for can train someone else — and replace you."

    — Naval

    • Found by pursuing your genuine curiosity and passion
    • Feels like play to you, looks like work to others
    • Cannot be outsourced or automated
    03
    Principle

    Build Leverage

    People
    Team working for you
    Money
    Capital working for you
    Content
    Media working for you
    Code
    Software working for you

    Code & content are best — permissionless leverage.

    04
    Principle

    Judgment

    "In an age of infinite leverage, judgment becomes the most important skill."

    — Naval

    • Direction matters more than speed
    • Wisdom applied to external problems
    • A calm mind makes better decisions
    05
    Principle

    Take Accountability

    "Embrace accountability, and take business risks under your own name."

    — Naval

    • Skin in the game builds reputation
    • Fail under your name → you get the critique
    • Win under your name → you get the credits
    06
    Principle

    Work That Feels Like Play

    "The goal is not to retire. It's to find work that feels like play to you but looks like work to others."

    — Naval

    When you're aligned, the barrier between work and play fades. This is the ultimate unfair advantage.

    07
    Principle

    Authentic Creation

    "Escape competition through authenticity."

    • The only fair comparison is with yourself
    • Nobody can compete with you at being you
    • Authenticity is the only way to win — especially with AI
    08
    Principle

    The Right Skillset

    Five high-leverage skills that compound across every field:

    • Writing
    • Reading
    • Arithmetic
    • Persuasion (talking)
    • Computer programming — "the closest thing to magic"
    09
    Principle

    Pick The Right Partners

    "Pick business partners with high intelligence, energy, and, above all, integrity."

    — Naval

    Intelligence
    Low → incompetence
    Energy
    Low → laziness
    Integrity
    Low → betrayal
    10
    Principle

    Stay Off The Hedonic Treadmill

    "People who live far below their means enjoy a freedom that people busy upgrading their lifestyles can't fathom."

    — Naval

    The goal of money isn't to buy stuff. It's to not have people buy your time.

    In Short

    "Learn to sell. Learn to build. If you can do both, you will be unstoppable."

    — Naval Ravikant
