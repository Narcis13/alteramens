## Framework Applied (Dan Koe)

Concepte cheie integrate:
1. **Anti-vision** = what's at stake (losing)
2. **Vision** = how you win
3. **1 year goal** = the mission
4. **1 month project** = boss fight
5. **Daily levers** = quests
6. **Constraints** = rules

---

## Key Insight
> "If you want a specific outcome in life, you must have the lifestyle that creates that outcome long before you reach it."

Nu e vorba de disciplină forțată, ci de a deveni persoana care natural face acele lucruri.
