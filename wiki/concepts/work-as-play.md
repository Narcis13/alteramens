# Work as Play — The Alignment Principle

> "The goal is not to retire. It's to find work that feels like play to you but looks like work to others." — Naval

## The Insight

The secret to consistency is alignment. When work feels like play, you can do it forever without needing vacations from it. The barrier between work and play dissolves.

Most people chase motivation. The best creators chase alignment.

## How to Find It

Think of activities that are effortless to you:
- You don't need to be convinced to do them
- You lose track of time doing them
- You could do them forever
- They energize rather than drain you

This is what work should feel like.

## Why It Matters for [[compounding-games]]

Compounding requires consistency over long periods. If work drains you, you'll stop. If work energizes you, you'll compound naturally.

## The Unfair Advantage

When your work is play:
- You outwork everyone because you never tire
- You develop [[specific-knowledge]] naturally
- The quality of your output increases (you care more)
- You become impossible to compete with

## Applied to Alteramens

The test: does building these products feel like play? The AI-augmented development with Claude Code — does that feel effortless? If yes, the alignment is right. If not, re-examine the direction.
