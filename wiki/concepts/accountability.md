# Accountability — Risks Under Your Own Name

Taking business risks under your own name. It gives you skin in the game and is how you build reputation.

> "Embrace accountability, and take business risks under your own name. Society will reward you with responsibility, equity, and leverage." — [[naval-ravikant|Naval]]

## The Double Edge

- **Upside:** Wins build credibility, trust, and equity
- **Downside:** Failures are public

Most people avoid accountability because of the downside. But this is exactly what makes it valuable — it's a scarce resource because most people are afraid of it.

## The Trust Equation

High integrity + genuine effort = people forgive failures. It's not about being perfect. It's about trying your best with integrity. When you do this, eventually you get rewarded.
