# X Content Pillars

**The 3 locked content pillars that govern Narcis's presence on platform X.** Every X post — whether generated via `/semnal-draft` (daily seeds), `/semnal-reply` (sweet-spot replies), or `/to-content` (multi-platform packs) — must declare which pilon it serves and respect that pilon's voice register.

This is the canonical wiki form. The operational working copy used by `semnal` day-to-day lives at `workshop/x-queue/pillars.md` — it mirrors this file but evolves faster (`/semnal-reflect` updates it weekly based on what's working). Promotions back here happen at milestone reviews or via `/faber-link`.

For the **mechanics** (hook, length, variants, lint) of writing on X, see [[x-voice-rules]]. For the strategic framework that produced these pillars, see [[content-pillars]].

---

## Selection filter (each pilon must pass 3 tests)

1. **Moat test** — could anyone else write it with the same authority? If yes → weak pilon.
2. **Sustainability test** — can Narcis write 2-3 posts/week indefinitely without burning out? If no → fragile pilon.
3. **Compounding test** — does one post send to another post, building authority over time? If no → flat pilon.

All three pillars below pass all three tests (validated 2026-04-21).

## Pilonul 1 — "AI-native craft" (WHAT)

> *Daily lab journal: AI agents, Claude Code, skills, judgment encoded in code.*

**Why only Narcis:** 30 years of code + active AI power user + writes skills for agents. 99% of people posting about AI don't write code. He does. The difference shows.

**Post types:**
- Micro case-study: "I solved X with skill Y in Z minutes — here's the pattern"
- Contrarian opinions on how agents are being used
- Concrete workflow demonstrations
- Observations about what AI does / doesn't do well yet

**Audience:** developers asking themselves how to use AI seriously, not just ChatGPT for boilerplate.

**Voice register on this pilon** (see [[x-voice-rules]] for full mechanics): **plain** dominates; **spicy** when taking a stance against a popular pattern. Avoid tutorial-speak — state the pattern/judgment, show you live with it.

**Cross-references:**
- [[skill-era]] — the underlying thesis
- [[dogfood-as-content]] — building Semnal *is* content for this pilon (meta-loop)
- [[compounding-games]] — long-game framing

## Pilonul 2 — "51-year-old builder" (WHO / WHY)

> *Atypical solopreneur journal. Reverse narrative vs the 22-yo-SF-founder template.*

**Why only Narcis:** rarity = memorability. In the AI space, few people lived through the 386 era. He did. The personally-lived technological arc is an asset juniors cannot fabricate.

**Post types:**
- Tech flashbacks (386 → 2026)
- What I learned at 30 that I'd tell my 20-year-old self
- The mental state of building post-career
- Real constraints (08-15 hospital job, Romania) → forced creativity

**Audience:** late-bloomers, career-changers, any builder who feels "too old" or "out of place". Plus those who appreciate authenticity in an ocean of founder-porn.

**Voice register:** **reflective** dominates. Flashback → reframe → punchy landing. Don't overdo nostalgia. Specific tech reference (Turbo Pascal, DOS 3.1) > "back in my day".

**Cross-references:**
- [[productize-yourself]] — Naval framework underlying the persona
- [[authentic-creation]] — why this works as differentiation

## Pilonul 3 — "Unsexy problems" (WHERE / WHAT-FOR)

> *Real SMB / public-system problems. Where leverage actually lives, far from the VC echo chamber.*

**Why only Narcis:** he has access to two laboratories SF Twitter doesn't see — (a) Romanian public hospital with 20 years of trenching, (b) ~20 SMB accounting clients via his wife. The market signal is brutally real.

**Post types:**
- Field observations ("today I saw X business losing Y hours/week on Z")
- Critique of SaaS hype vs SMB reality
- Product opportunities nobody is discussing
- The brutality of the public system as a constraint laboratory

**Audience:** pragmatic builders who care about real revenue, not vanity metrics. Potential → future customers for what Narcis builds.

**Voice register:** **spicy** dominates. Specific observation > generic critique. Name the unsexy thing (ANAF report, Excel reconciliation, hospital procurement form). Abstraction kills the angle.

**Cross-references:**
- [[productize-yourself]] — specific knowledge from access
- [[skill-era]] — the unsexy is where encoded judgment scales

## Rotation rule

- **Minimum 1 post per pilon per week** (no pilon goes silent for >7 days)
- **Target: 5-7 posts/week** → ideal distribution `2/2/2 + 1 wildcard` (reply, thread, or meta)
- **Avoid 3 consecutive posts from the same pilon** (timeline fatigue)

`/semnal-draft` and `/to-content` (when generating the X piece) should consult the working copy at `workshop/x-queue/pillars.md` and the recent contents of `workshop/x-queue/published/` to bias pilon selection toward the under-served pilon of the week.

## Cross-pillar interaction notes

1. **Pilon 1 + [[dogfood-as-content]] feed each other.** Building Semnal (the tool) generates organic Pillar 1 content. The meta-loop is self-sustaining.
2. **Pilon 2 is the only "awareness-led" pilon.** It makes the account memorable, not just read. Rarity = mnemonic device for both algorithm and followers.
3. **Pilon 3 is the cleanest compounding bet.** Encoded judgment, perfectly aligned with Alteramens philosophy. Lower short-term engagement than pilon 2; highest long-term defensibility.

All three satisfy: aligned with the product (for Narcis, the product = Narcis; [[productize-yourself]]), owned by authenticity ([[authentic-creation]]), sustainable cadence (build + hospital + Romania = inexhaustible material).
