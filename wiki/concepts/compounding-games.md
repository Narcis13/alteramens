# Compounding Games — Play Long-Term

> "All the returns in life, whether in wealth, relationships, or knowledge, come from compound interest." — Naval

## The Principle

Pick an industry, craft, and people you want to play with long-term. Every direction change interrupts compounding. Growth is slow at first, then accelerates exponentially. The key is to not interrupt the process.

## Why Most People Fail

They chase shiny objects. They measure in days, not decades. They change direction too often, resetting the compound interest clock.

## Alteramens as a Compounding Game

The game: building software products that encode specific knowledge.
- The industry: B2B SaaS / DevTools for Romanian market
- The craft: AI-augmented development
- The people: Claude Code as partner, wife as market validator, future customers

Every project, every source ingested, every decision documented compounds. The wiki itself is a compounding mechanism — knowledge gets richer with every addition.

## Anti-Pattern: Direction Changes

Each abandoned project resets the clock. The Manifest is explicit: [[kill-fast]] applies to ideas without signs of life, but the overall GAME (building profitable software) shouldn't change.
