# Making $$ with AI Marketing — 7 Distribution Strategies

Thread from The [[startup-ideas-podcast]] (@startupideaspod), 2026-03-30.

## Core Thesis

In the age of vibe coding, building is no longer the bottleneck — distribution is. Hundreds of thousands of projects launch daily, most to zero users. The winning move is not a better product but a better distribution strategy.

This directly reinforces [[distribution-over-product]] and connects to the Alteramens principle of [[validate-before-build]].

## The Seven Strategies

### 1. MCP Servers as Sales Team
Build plugins for AI assistants that funnel users to your product. Zero-cost customer acquisition through agent workflows. See [[mcp-as-distribution]].

### 2. Programmatic SEO
Generate 10,000+ pages targeting long-tail keyword patterns. Automated content at scale for passive organic traffic. See [[programmatic-seo]].

### 3. Free Tool as Top of Funnel
Create a free, shareable tool that delivers real value. The tool itself drives word-of-mouth and conversions to paid.

### 4. Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)
Optimize to be the source that Perplexity, ChatGPT, and other AI tools cite in their answers. The new SEO for the AI era. See [[answer-engine-optimization]].

### 5. Viral Artifacts
Design shareable outputs (like Spotify Wrapped) that users distribute on your behalf. The product creates its own marketing.  See [[viral-artifacts]].

### 6. Buy a Newsletter
Acquire an existing audience for $5K–$20K. Skip the audience-building phase entirely. A capital-for-distribution trade.

### 7. AI Content Repurposing
Convert one pillar content piece into tweets, videos, short-form posts. Maximum reach from minimum creation. [[leverage]] through content multiplication.

## Tactical Advice

Pick two strategies. Start this week. The bias for action is explicit — stop building, start distributing.
