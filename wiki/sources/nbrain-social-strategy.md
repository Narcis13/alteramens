# nbrAIn Social Media & Brand Strategy

Go-to-market strategy for the nbrAIn brand. Covers positioning, platform choice, content pillars, and LinkedIn/TikTok setup. Source: [[strategies/social-media-plan|Strategy doc]].

## Brand Identity

**Name:** nbrAIn (pronounced "brain")
- Brain = intelligence, thinking
- AI visually highlighted (nbr**AI**n)
- nb = Narcis Brindusescu (personal)

**Tagline:** "Datele tale, la o întrebare distanță" (Your data, one question away)

## Positioning

**Not:** "Developer who makes SaaS"
**Yes:** "The economist who automates accounting with AI"

Narrative: 10 years in accounting → saw how accountants struggle → building AI that gives them time back → building in public.

## Content Strategy

Five pillars:
1. Behind the scenes — "Today I built X"
2. Entrepreneur education — "3 things your accountant wants you to know"
3. AI in business — "How I use AI to automate Y"
4. Founder challenges — "I have 4 hours/day. Here's how I use them"
5. Public validation — "I asked 10 entrepreneurs about Z"

## Constraints

- No face-on-camera (for now)
- Limited time: dead periods at work + evenings
- LinkedIn for professional audience, TikTok for younger entrepreneurs
