# Alteramens Manifest

The founding document of Alteramens. Contains the AntiVision (what to refuse), the Vision (what to build toward), principles, timeline, daily operational rhythms, and constraints. Source: [[MANIFEST]].

## AntiVision (What Drives Action)

Not theoretical — visceral. Driven by refusal to:
- Waste peak intellectual years in bureaucratic inertia
- Compromise integrity (lying, stealing, complacency)
- Fall behind economically relative to spouse
- Hide from the market while polishing unused tools
- Miss daily opportunities during the most productive hours
- Fail to model ambition for children

## Vision

Freedom: control over time, location, and work. Home gym, proper tech setup, mountain property, business portfolio generating passive income, travel with wife, spiritual depth.

## Five Principles

1. **Start small** — A small executed idea beats a big imagined one
2. **Validate first** — Before code, validate the problem with real people
3. **Revenue > Vanity** — Only what generates revenue matters
4. **Ship weekly** — Regular cadence forces progress
5. **Kill fast** — 2-3 weeks without life signs → archive and next

## Timeline (Jan-Jul 2026)

Jan: Setup + 3 ideas → Feb: First experiment → Mar: First users → Apr: First payment → May: 100$ MRR → Jun: 500$ MRR → Jul: 1000$ MRR

## Daily Levers

- Morning 06:00-07:30: 1h deep work, zero distractions
- Evening 16:00-18:00: 30min review + 30min market interaction
- Weekend: 2h Saturday build + 2h Sunday reflection

## Non-Negotiable Constraints

Family, health, faith, day job — these don't bend for Alteramens. The golden rule: if a decision violates a non-negotiable to advance Alteramens, the decision is wrong.
