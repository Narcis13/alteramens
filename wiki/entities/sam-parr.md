# Sam Parr

Founder of The Hustle (acquired by HubSpot) and co-host of "My First Million." Cited in [[pat-walls-agent-first-1t-thread]] as one of the founders who already understands that the media layer IS the distribution layer for the next wave of startups.

## Relevance

Proof point for the "media + agents = new SaaS" thesis. Parr built a media property first, sold it, and now operates with the audience as permanent infrastructure. This is the inverse of the old pattern (build product → bolt on marketing) and maps directly to [[media-plus-agents-distribution]].

## Why He's Cited Here

The thread argues Silicon Valley is going influencer because distribution is the moat when everyone can wire agents to APIs. Parr is named alongside [[pat-walls]] and [[roy-lee]] as founders already operating on this axis. The citation is a signal for *who to learn from*, not a detailed profile.

## Why This Matters for Alteramens

Same reference model as [[pat-walls]]: audience-first founder distribution is validated as a strategy, not a nice-to-have. Relevant to [[personal-brand-strategy]] and [[nbrain-social-strategy]].
