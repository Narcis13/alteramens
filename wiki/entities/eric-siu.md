# Eric Siu

CEO of [[single-grain]], a marketing agency rebuilding itself around AI agents. One of the first to publicly document implementing Jack Dorsey's "From Hierarchy to Intelligence" framework at a real company.

## Relevance

Practitioner, not theorist. Running an AI-native org since late December 2025, publishing implementation notes openly. Represents the archetype of a small company CEO using AI agents for massive leverage — resonates with [[skill-era]] thesis.

## Key Contributions

- Built [[single-brain]] — unified vector DB as company-wide "world model"
- Deployed named agent fleet (Alfred, Arrow, Oracle, Flash, Cyborg) with World Agent coordinator
- Documented the compounding curve honestly: Month 1 terrible → Month 3 flywheel → Month 4 different company
- Identified that the gap between Dorsey's theory and implementation is itself a business opportunity
- Pioneered the "internal system → product" model for agencies

## Notable Claim

The internal AI operating system becomes the product you sell to clients. Your months of mistakes and learnings are the differentiation.
