# Pat Walls

Founder of Starter Story and host of [[startup-ideas-podcast]]. Builds his audience as the primary distribution surface for the bootstrapper / indie founder archetype. Prolific publisher of thread-form essays about the shift from SaaS to agent-native companies.

## Relevance

Exemplar of the **creator-founder** pattern the thread itself argues is winning: audience-first, short-form, opinionated writing used as both a filter (attracts operators who match the thesis) and a distribution channel (any new product ships with built-in demand).

## Notable Claims

- $1T of value is up for grabs across 10,000+ agent-first niches in the next cycle — see [[pat-walls-agent-first-1t-thread]].
- Ex-operators with deep vertical workflow knowledge beat code-first founders because the insight is the moat, not the implementation — see [[vertical-operator-edge]].
- Distribution (audience + brand) becomes the defensibility layer when agents make building commodity — see [[media-plus-agents-distribution]].

## Why This Matters for Alteramens

Walls operates the exact playbook the Alteramens strategy is converging on: personal brand + content + an opinionated worldview, productized through tools and media. Reference model for [[nbrain-social-strategy]] and [[personal-brand-strategy]].
