# UMF Carol Davila — Universitatea de Medicină și Farmacie București

The largest medical university in Romania, located in Bucharest. The most competitive medicine faculty in the country and the primary admission target for the AI tutor product wedge.

## Why It Matters to Alteramens

Carol Davila is the **bounded target** for the AI tutor for medical admission product. It is not interesting because it is prestigious — it is interesting because it is **specific, measurable, and surrounded by paying parents**:

- Specific manual: Manual de Biologie clasa XI-XII, Editura Didactică
- Specific exam format: grilă, complement simplu și complement multiplu
- Specific barem (scoring rules), publicly known
- Specific deadline: admission held annually (typically July)
- Approximately 10,000 candidates per year for approximately 600 medicine general spots
- Existing market: meditații at 100-200 lei/h for 6-12 months pre-exam
- Parents pay 5,000-10,000 EUR per cohort for admission preparation

Every one of these properties is what makes the product viable. None of them exist for "personalized learning platform" in general.

## Role in Product Strategy

- **Wedge:** the first vertical the AI tutor targets, with all content built specifically for it
- **Validation target:** Mihai applies here in 2027, providing the live proof point
- **Compounding base:** content and engineering built for Carol Davila extends naturally to other UMF (Cluj, Iași, Timișoara, Tg Mureș), then medical schools across Eastern Europe, eventually USMLE/MIR

## Subjects in Scope

The admission exam typically covers:
- Biologie (Anatomie + Genetică, from manual XI + XII)
- Chimie organică sau Fizică (candidate's choice)

Initial product wedge focuses on **biologie** because (a) it is the most universal choice, (b) it has the clearest content scope, and (c) Mihai will take it.
