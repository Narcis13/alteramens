# Workday

Enterprise HR, finance, and planning platform — the dominant "system of record" for large-company HCM and ERP workflows. Named in [[pat-walls-agent-first-1t-thread]] alongside [[salesforce]] and [[hubspot]] as a candidate "dumb backend" in the agent-native era.

## Why It Matters Here

Workday is the cleanest example of the thread's thesis: it is notoriously interface-heavy, implementation-heavy, and consultant-heavy. A typical deployment costs 7-figures in professional services. The argument is that agent-native startups will *completely replace* this implementation layer:

- Today: Workday + 18 months of consulting = working HR system
- Tomorrow: Workday API + vertical agent = working HR system, zero consulting

This is the cleanest expression of [[agent-replaces-implementation]].

## Relevance for Alteramens

Not directly relevant to the near-term Romanian market focus, but the pattern generalizes:
- Anywhere there's a "system of record + huge services tax" is a candidate niche for an agent-native wedge.
- Healthcare IT (Narcis's day-job domain) has analogous dynamics — the hospital HIS / EMR systems carry massive implementation costs that agent-native tools could compress.
