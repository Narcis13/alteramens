# Jack Dorsey

Co-founder of Twitter/X and CEO of [[block-inc]]. Co-authored "From Hierarchy to Intelligence" with Roelof Botha (Sequoia) on April 1, 2026.

## Relevance

Published what people are calling the most important organizational design document since the Toyota Production System. The essay proposes replacing middle management coordination layers with AI — a 4-layer framework for AI-native companies. Block is implementing this at 6,000-person scale.

## The "From Hierarchy to Intelligence" Framework

Four layers: Capabilities (raw AI tools) → World Model (company's living memory) → Intelligence Layer (dynamic decision-making) → Surfaces (human interaction points).

The essay advocates cutting coordination roles and replacing them with AI that holds the full organizational context. DRI (Directly Responsible Individual) system for project execution.

## Connection to Alteramens

The framework validates the [[skill-era]] thesis: small teams with AI leverage operate at fundamentally different speed. The "world model" concept parallels Faber's approach — compounding knowledge that can't be fast-forwarded.
