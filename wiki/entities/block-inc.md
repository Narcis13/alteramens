# Block, Inc.

Fintech company (Cash App, Square) led by [[jack-dorsey]]. Implementing the "From Hierarchy to Intelligence" framework at 6,000-person scale — cutting middle management and replacing coordination layers with AI.

## AI-Native Implementation

Running two "world models": one for internal operations, one for customer behavior. AI routes transactions, composes financial products, coordinates across Cash App and Square without human middle managers.

Uses DRI (Directly Responsible Individual) system for project execution.

## Relevance

Largest-scale known implementation of [[ai-native-org-design]]. If Block can do this at 6,000 people, smaller companies have fewer obstacles. Validates the thesis at enterprise scale.
