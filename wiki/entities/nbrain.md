# nbrAIn — Agentic Business Platform

The primary revenue candidate for [[alteramens]]. A B2B SaaS for Romanian accountants and SME entrepreneurs.

**Brand:** nbrAIn (pronounced "brain" — contains AI, nb = Narcis Brindusescu)
**Tagline:** "Datele tale, la o întrebare distanță"

## What It Does

Conversational AI interface replacing rigid accounting forms. Accountants and entrepreneurs share data and interact through natural language questions instead of preset reports.

## Market

- **Target:** Romanian independent accountants / small firms (1-5 people) + SME entrepreneurs
- **Size:** ~8,000 accounting firms in Romania
- **Price:** 50 EUR/month per firm (~98% margin)
- **Path to 1K MRR:** 10-34 paying firms

## Validation Status

Wife interview completed (Jan 2026). Confirmed the pain: time stress, chaotic document delivery, repetitive client questions. Next: demo with 2-3 real clients.

## Tech

Built on [[workscript]] engine (75% ready). Migrations to PostgreSQL/NextJS deferred until after validation.

## Strategic Position

This is where [[specific-knowledge]] meets [[leverage]] meets [[accountability]]. Narcis's dual background (economist + developer) + wife's client access = unfair advantages that compound.
