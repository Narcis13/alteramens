# nbrAIn Market Intelligence — What We Know

A synthesis of everything accumulated about the nbrAIn market opportunity. Status: early validation.

## Confirmed (High Confidence)

From wife interview (Jan 2026):
- **Pain exists:** Accountants don't have enough time to finish all firms
- **Document chaos:** Clients send docs via email, WhatsApp, physical delivery, sometimes literally thrown over the fence
- **Repetitive questions:** "How much do I owe ANAF?" and "How much can I take as dividends?" are the top recurring questions
- **All tasks similar:** Every monthly task takes approximately the same time (no easy wins)
- **Clients want visibility:** They want to see "how they stand" without calling

## Assumed (Medium Confidence)

- Romanian market has ~8,000 accounting firms
- Price point: 50 EUR/month per firm is acceptable
- 10-34 paying firms = 1K MRR target
- API cost ~$1/user/month → ~98% margin
- No competitor offers conversational AI in this niche

## Unknown (Gaps to Fill)

- **Would accountants PAY for this?** (vs. using it if free) — NOT YET VALIDATED
- **What other questions do clients ask?** (beyond ANAF and dividends)
- **What's the sales cycle?** (how long from demo to payment)
- **What's the churn risk?** (is this a "nice to have" that gets cancelled?)
- **Competitor landscape:** FGO, Facturis, SmartBill — what exactly do they offer?
- **How accurate must AI answers be?** (accounting requires precision — errors are costly)
- **Regulatory risk:** Does ANAF change rules frequently enough to break integrations?

## Next Steps (Validation Priorities)

1. Demo with 2-3 of wife's clients (Wizard of Oz approach)
2. Ask explicitly: "Would you pay 50 EUR/month for this?"
3. Identify the top 10 questions they'd ask
4. Research FGO/SmartBill feature sets
5. Map ANAF API stability and limitations

## Maturity Assessment

We're at **"Problem validated, solution assumed."** The pain is real. The willingness to pay is unproven. This is the most critical gap to close.
