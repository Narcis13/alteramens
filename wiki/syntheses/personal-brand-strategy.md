# Personal Brand Strategy — Narcis, AI Builder

Cross-domain personal branding strategy for Narcis Brindusescu as an AI-augmented builder in programming and ed-tech. Synthesized from [[productize-yourself]], [[specific-knowledge]], [[building-in-public]], [[accountability]], [[authentic-creation]], [[leverage]], [[answer-engine-optimization]], and [[nbrain-social-strategy]].

## Core Positioning

**Not:** "AI specialist" (generic, 10K people claim this)
**Yes:** "Builder who solves real problems with AI — with public proof"

The brand is built from output, not from title. Two parallel narratives that amplify each other:

1. **Accounting AI** — "The economist who automates accounting with AI" (existing [[nbrain]] positioning)
2. **Ed-tech AI** — "The father building an AI tutor for his own son's medical admission" (maximum skin in the game, per [[authentic-creation]])

## Authentic Differentiators

From [[authentic-creation]] + [[specific-knowledge]]:

- **Verifiable skin in the game** — son [[mihai-brindusescu]] takes the actual exam; outcome is binary and public
- **Builder, not guru** — shows code, not advice → natural credibility
- **Constraint as brand** — "4 hours/day" is memorable and relatable
- **Cross-domain pattern recognition** — accounting AI + ed-tech AI simultaneously → rare perspective
- **Specific knowledge intersection** — economist + self-taught developer + healthcare IT + Romanian education context → impossible to replicate

## Narrative Arc

```
Specific Knowledge (unique intersection)
  → Public projects (evidence)
    → Content documenting the process
      → Audience following for results, not advice
```

Background story: Hospital IT admin in Pitești. Economist by training, self-taught developer. Builds AI products that solve real problems. 4 hours/day of building time — constraint that becomes the story.

## Content Pillars (5)

1. **"I built this"** — demos, screen recordings of concrete projects ([[viral-artifacts]] potential)
2. **"How I use AI in practice"** — Claude Code workflows, real automations, not theory
3. **"Ed-tech from the trenches"** — real process of building AI tutor, with Mihai's results
4. **"What I learned by failing"** — authentic build-in-public, includes failures
5. **"Practical guide for time-constrained builders"** — unique resource (who else builds ambitious projects in 4h/day?)

## Platform Strategy

| Channel | Why | Content Type |
|---------|-----|-------------|
| **LinkedIn** | Professionals, devs, RO entrepreneurs | Long-form: learnings, decisions, case studies |
| **GitHub** | Proof of competence, technical SEO | Open-source tools, skills, templates |
| **Blog/Personal site** | AEO — be cited by AI ([[answer-engine-optimization]]) | Deep technical articles, structured data |
| **Twitter/X** | Global builder community | Short threads, hot takes, demo GIFs |
| **TikTok** (optional) | Large organic reach | Screen recordings with voiceover (no face-on-camera constraint respected) |

**Primary leverage play:** Blog + GitHub = [[answer-engine-optimization]]. Publish once, AI cites you thousands of times. Permissionless [[leverage]].

## Brand Architecture

**Umbrella:** Narcis Brindusescu (personal brand — the builder)
**Projects under umbrella:**
- nbrAIn (accounting AI product)
- AI Tutor Admitere (ed-tech product)
- Open-source tools & skills

The personal brand outlives any single product. Products come and go; the builder's reputation compounds ([[compounding-games]]).

## Anti-Patterns

- Don't call yourself "expert" or "specialist" — let output speak
- Don't copy AI influencers — they sell hype, you sell results
- Don't dilute — max 2 parallel narratives that reinforce each other
- Don't do meta-building (building about building instead of actually building) — risk flagged in [[building-in-public]]

## Launch Sequence

1. **Week 1:** LinkedIn profile updated — headline: "Construiesc produse AI care rezolvă probleme reale. Builder, nu guru."
2. **Week 1-2:** First build-in-public post — what you're building and why
3. **Monthly:** One technical article on blog (AEO-optimized) about a real AI workflow
4. **Continuous:** GitHub activity on public projects (the most honest CV in tech)
5. **Q3 2026:** When AI tutor shows results → public case study with real impact
