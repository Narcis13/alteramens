# Growth Tweet Set — 2026-05 W21

A 28-tweet week (4/day × 7 days) for `@13_narcissus` on X — identity-first reflection of who Narcis is, what he wants to be, what he knows, what he's assimilated. EN-dominant, no emoji / hashtag / mention, voice-rules locked, each tweet tagged with target pillar.

> Recovered after a power-cut interrupted the original session on 2026-05-22.

## Optimization Levers

- **Hook tweets** (1, 9, 13, 17, 21, 25) — contrarian, quotable, RT-bait
- **Spec tweets** — concrete numbers (51, 386, 1991, 20 years, 4,000 transactions, 12 layers, 30%, 6 months)
- **Identity tweets** — pillar 2 + bio anchor placed in the first scrolls
- **Reply-bait opens** (#4, #14, #22, #26) — invite quotes without bait language
- 4 tweets/day × 7 days, mix per day (hook / spec / identity / reply-bait)

## Growth Positioning Notes

- **Hook days** (1, 3, 5, 7) lead with contrarian or quotable claims for broad RT/quote surface.
- **Specificity tweets** (5, 8, 11, 15, 27) anchor with concrete numbers — satisfies `specific-numbers-concrete-examples` voice rule and rewards screenshotters.
- **Identity tweets** (3, 19, 28) reinforce bio + niche so new followers from hook tweets understand the brand within 4 scrolls.
- **Reply-bait opens** (#4, #14, #22, #26) — designed to attract quote-tweets from larger niche accounts without bait language.
- **No hashtags, no emojis, no @mentions** — keeps texture consistent.

## Risks & Watch Points

- **#7 risk:** "Median builder" framing can read as dismissive of younger founders. Hold tone with empathy on quote-replies.
- **#11 dependency:** assumes `semnal-draft` skill is shipped and verifiable. If not, swap to a different concrete skill or hold.
- **#15 dependency:** the 4,000 transactions/month figure must be real (Adriana's clients). Verify before posting.
- **#27 trap:** "52 artifacts" is a public commitment. Don't soften it — let it bind.
